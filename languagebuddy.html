About Language Buddy

The idea for Language Buddy comes from my passion for learning languages. I am currently learning Japanese and Vietnamese and while I can organize how I learn on a separate notepad, there are many "novelties" that would be nice. Some ideas I have are to implement the meetup/google maps API to show me where the meetups are in my area for certain language learning groups.

In addition, I'm at different stages of learning for each language and require different methods of studying. It would be nice just to have one place where I can place/update all my resources.
