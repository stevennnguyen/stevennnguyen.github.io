About Me

My name is Steven Nguyen and I’m a QA Specialist with 4 years of experience working in the retail, real estate, and printer management industries. I graduated from UCI in March of 2017 with a BS in Informatics.

I like the variety that the QA field has to offer with plenty of opportunity to branch out into more specialized roles. Long term, I’m looking to get into a team lead or managerial position. In the mean time, I’m honing my skills at work and through personal projects.

Outside of work, my hobbies include learning foreign languages, DJing, ultimate frisbee, disc golf, reading, camping, and hiking.
