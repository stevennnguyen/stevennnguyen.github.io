About Me

My name is Steven Nguyen and I was born and raised in Oxnard, California but currently residing here in Irvine. I am a University of California, Irvine graduate with a BS in Informatics. I chose informatics for it's human centered design curriculum. What I've learned from the major is reflected in the work that I do. I love programming simple, functional apps that solve everyday problems. I am most experienced in front end development, but I don't hesitate to try new things like back end programming, mobile applications, UX design, software design, and project management.

Into self improvement, consciousness, and all things that better your life. Reading, meditation, gym, hiking, learning languages (Japanese, Vietnamese, Chinese), music, cinematics, and cooking (dumplings) are just a few of the things I do when I'm not programming.
